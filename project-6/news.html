FALL REGIONALS

During our Fall 2025 season, we went 6-0 in order to cinch a spot at the Regional Playoffs. Together our team traveled to Ohio and competed against Marquette University in the round of 16. Unfortunately we lost our first game which resulted in a consellation game for 3rd place in the region. We faced Bowling Green and came out on top, securing our first post season 15s win!
